A BALANCE OF PURPOSE AND PROFIT

THE FIRST EYEWE

B-CORP IN THE UK

In April 2020 we became the first UK eyewear company to become a certified B Corp.

For us this is about making a public statement that we are a force for good.

SUSTAINABLE MATERIALS

Our premium frames use materials including certified woods, bio-based acetate and repurposed aerospace aluminium. Founded by three brothers from Devon, UK, our aim is to drive change in the eyewear industry by designing beautiful frames that both look good and do good.
